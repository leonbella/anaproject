Movement Never Lies

Although body movement is the common denominator of all human endeavor, it is ephemeral. It appears for an instant and then disappears, leaving only a trace in the mind of the observer. You can, however, learn to understand your movement and its inner and outer manifestations using the Laban Movement Analysis. Movement can be divided into four components: Body, Effort, Space and Shape in order to observe, analyze and re-pattern movement scientifically and efficiently. AB Coaching can help you better understand how and why you move to help you reach your fullest potential whether in business, fitness or for health.

Health & Fitness





Create, develop and teach exercise programs. Develop health and wellness lecture demonstrations focused on the somatic principles. Somatic movement is about awakening the awareness of the human body in motion. Empowering healing, well-being and harmonious movement.

Dance Education





Instruction in dance and movement specializing in Laban Movement Analysis (LMA), Bartenieff Fundamentals (BF), and Motif Notation. LMA, BF, and Motif Notation are based on the work of dance pioneer Rudolf Laban and his Protégé Irmgard Bartenieff.

About Ana


Ana is an independent consultant in movement empowerment, based in New York City. She is a Laban Certified Movement Analyst (CMA), a Registered Somatic Movement Therapist/Educator (RSMT/E) and Certified Fitness Expert and Moving For Life Certified Instructor (MFLCI). She holds a Masters Degree in Dance Education from New York University and a Bachelor of Arts from Cornell University.

Ana would love to work with you in one of three diverse but related areas: Embodied Leadership, Dance and Movement Education or in Health and Fitness.
